Tchektchuoka receipe

This is a very quick preparated food at home, very easy to prepare, I like it because I enjoy it's preparation and it's taste also; and specially when I do not have time

  • Eggs
  • Totato
  1. Cut tomatoes and put then in the pam for 3 minutes in a strong fire🍅
  2. Break the eggs directly on the tomatoes🥚
  3. Let for 2 minutes then mix gently🍳
  4. Add some salt🧂
  5. Serve hot with bread.🍞
